  • Breeding Bin: FF-BIN-B03-L5-P3 (Rack B03, Level 5, Position 3)
  • Grow-Out Bin: FF-BIN-G02-L4-P2 (Rack G02, Level 4, Position 2)
  • Maternity Bin: FF-BIN-M01-L2-P5 (Rack M01, Level 2, Position 5)
  • Breeder: FF-BREEDER-B01-F042 (Rack B01, Female 42) or FF-BREEDER-B01-M012 (Male 12)
  • Batch: FF-BATCH-2024-0001 (Year-Sequential)
